Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a position at MD Groups?
You can apply directly through the application form on this page or send your resume to msnciviltraders@gmail.com with the role name in the subject line. We review all applications personally.
How long does the hiring process take?
Our typical hiring process takes 2–3 weeks from application to offer. This includes an initial review (5–7 days), a brief phone screening, and a final interview. We keep you informed at every step.
Can I apply if I don't see a relevant opening listed?
Absolutely! Select "General / Open Application" in the form or email us your resume. We maintain a talent pool and reach out when a suitable role opens up. We actively look for good people across all our ventures.
Are there opportunities for freshers / recent graduates?
Yes! We welcome freshers especially for roles in admin, marketing, real estate sales, and farm management. We believe in building talent from within and offer guidance and mentorship to help new joiners grow quickly.
What documents do I need for the application?
For the initial application, just your resume (PDF or Word format) and a brief cover note are sufficient. Additional documents like educational certificates, ID proofs, and experience letters will be requested at the offer stage.
Are the roles based in Tamil Nadu only?
Most of our roles are based in and around Pannur, Sriperumbudur, and Chennai — all in Tamil Nadu. Some marketing and digital roles may be hybrid or remote. Location details are mentioned on each job listing.
