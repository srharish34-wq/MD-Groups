Our Legacy

A Foundation Built on Agriculture, Strengthened by Vision

Mr. Sebastian Naidu, rooted in agriculture, established St. Joseph Rice Mills in 1952 — laying the groundwork for a venture defined by quality, trust, and service to the farming community.

His son, Mr. M. S. Mariadoss, transformed the mill into St. Joseph's Modern Rice Mills, introducing advanced systems and operational excellence that gained wide recognition across the Thiruvallur region.

In 2004, Mr. Mariadoss expanded into real estate, developing multiple residential layouts across Sriperumbudur, Kanchipuram, and Poonamallee — blending traditional values with forward-thinking execution.

The Visionary Behind MD Groups

Meet Our Founder

Mr. M. S. Mariadoss · Founder

A rare blend of traditional values and forward-thinking execution — evolving from agriculture to industrial and real estate development, while consistently creating opportunities and building trust.

Mr. M. S. Mariadoss inherited a vision from his father and transformed it into a modern, multi-sector enterprise. Under his leadership, St. Joseph's Modern Rice Mills gained wide recognition across the Thiruvallur region for exceptional service and consistency — becoming a trusted institution that supported farmers and generated meaningful employment.

A visionary influence in the rice milling industry, his approach and foresight served as inspiration to many new mill owners who looked up to him as a guiding figure. In 2004, he expanded into real estate, bringing the same principles of discipline, planning, and integrity to developing residential layouts across Sriperumbudur, Kanchipuram, and Poonamallee.

Today, under his continued guidance alongside his sons Mr. Joy Sebastian and Dr. Philip Roy, the group is stronger than ever — building industrial parks, real estate communities, and agricultural brands that carry forward the family's extraordinary legacy.

Joy & Roy Gardens

Four Generations of Pure, Honest Farming

In the fertile landscapes of Pannur and Moolapattu, Joy & Roy Gardens was born from a simple yet powerful idea — to cultivate not just fruits, but purity, quality, and trust. Rooted in tradition, the farms have been lovingly nurtured for four generations.

Our gardens are home to a rich selection of mangoes, sapotas, lemons, and jackfruit — each variety cultivated for superior taste and natural richness. We follow a philosophy of slow, responsible cultivation, allowing nature to take its course so that every fruit reaches its fullest potential in flavor, texture, and nourishment.

More than a farm, Joy & Roy Gardens is a legacy — of heritage, sustainability, and excellence. From four generations of care, to your table — pure, honest, and exceptional.
