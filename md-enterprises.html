About M.D. Enterprises

Industrial Infrastructure Built for Growth

M.D. Enterprises is a forward-looking industrial infrastructure provider dedicated to delivering high-quality, compliant, and scalable warehouse solutions in emerging manufacturing hubs of Tamil Nadu.

Strategically located in Pannur village, within the fast-growing Sriperumbudur–Oragadam industrial corridor, the company is ideally positioned to support a wide spectrum of manufacturing and logistics operations — backed by strong connectivity and a thriving industrial ecosystem.

The foundation of this industrial park has been shaped by the vision of Mr. M. S. Mariadoss, who has been instrumental in conceptualizing and developing the project from the ground up. Under his guidance, and in close collaboration with Mr. Joy Sebastian, the warehouses have been meticulously planned and executed to meet the evolving needs of modern industry.
