About the Project

A Community Built for Families & Investors

Jabamalai Avenue is a thoughtfully developed premium residential plotted community located in the fast-growing area of Pannur — offering the perfect balance between serene living and urban accessibility.

Designed for those seeking a peaceful yet well-connected lifestyle, the project offers premium residential plots with clear titles and a strong focus on quality infrastructure. Both Phase 1 and Phase 2 are positioned in a rapidly appreciating locality — making them suitable both for building your dream home and for long-term investment.

The project benefits from its strategic location within close proximity to the Sriperumbudur–Oragadam industrial corridor, ensuring strong future growth potential. Wide internal roads, organized layout planning, and a calm residential environment make Jabamalai Avenue a perfect choice for families looking to settle in a promising neighbourhood.

Why This Location

Location Advantages

Jabamalai Avenue's location in Pannur offers a rare combination of peaceful residential living and exceptional connectivity — making it a smart choice for both homebuilders and investors.

Near Don Bosco School

Located in close proximity to Don Bosco School Pannur — one of the region's most sought-after educational institutions — making it ideal for families with school-going children.

Industrial Corridor Access

Strategically positioned near the Sriperumbudur–Oragadam industrial corridor — home to global manufacturers — ensuring strong employment proximity and long-term land appreciation.

Highway Connectivity

Easy access to National Highway and key arterial roads — connecting residents efficiently to Chennai, Kanchipuram, and surrounding urban centres for work and lifestyle needs.

Calm Residential Environment

Away from the noise and congestion of the city, Pannur offers a serene, clean, and green living environment — perfect for families seeking quality of life alongside urban convenience.

Rapidly Appreciating Area

With the continued expansion of the Sriperumbudur industrial ecosystem and growing residential demand in Pannur, land values in this area are consistently appreciating year on year.

Growing Community

Pannur is emerging as a preferred residential destination for professionals and families working in the Sriperumbudur–Oragadam belt — creating a strong, thriving neighbourhood community.
