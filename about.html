Our Legacy

Rooted in Agriculture, Grown Through Vision

At the foundation of our journey lies a legacy shaped by agriculture, strengthened by innovation, and carried forward through visionary leadership.

Mr. Sebastian Naidu, coming from a strong agricultural background, established St. Joseph Rice Mills in 1952, laying the groundwork for a venture rooted in quality, trust, and service to the farming community of Tamil Nadu.

Building upon this foundation, his son, Mr. M. S. Mariadoss, emerged as a driving force who transformed the business into a modern and forward-looking enterprise. Under his leadership, the mill was upgraded into St. Joseph's Modern Rice Mills, where he introduced advanced systems, improved processes, and top-tier operational practices — significantly elevating the scale, efficiency, and quality of the business.

Mr. Mariadoss gained wide recognition across the Thiruvallur region for exceptional service, consistency, and reliability — becoming a trusted institution that supported farmers, generated employment, and contributed meaningfully to the local economy. His approach, foresight, and commitment to excellence served as an inspiration to many new rice mill owners who looked up to him as a guiding figure in the field.

With a natural entrepreneurial progression, Mr. Mariadoss expanded his vision beyond agriculture and entered the real estate sector in 2004 — developing multiple residential layouts across Sriperumbudur, Kanchipuram, and Poonamallee, while maintaining the same principles of discipline, planning, and integrity.

"A legacy led by vision, built on values, and defined by progress." — MD Groups

Meet Our Founder & Chairman

Mr. M. S. Mariadoss · Founder

A rare blend of traditional values and forward-thinking execution — evolving from agriculture to industrial and real estate development, while consistently creating opportunities and building trust.

Mr. M. S. Mariadoss inherited a vision from his father Mr. Sebastian Naidu and transformed it into a modern, multi-sector enterprise. Under his leadership, St. Joseph's Modern Rice Mills gained wide recognition across the Thiruvallur region for exceptional service, consistency, and reliability — becoming a trusted institution that supported farmers, generated employment, and contributed meaningfully to the local economy.

A visionary influence within the rice milling industry, his approach, foresight, and commitment to excellence served as an inspiration to many new rice mill owners who looked up to him as a guiding figure in the field. His ability to combine practical experience with deep commitment to compliance, quality, and long-term value creation has been the cornerstone of the group's success.

In 2004, Mr. Mariadoss expanded into real estate, bringing the same principles of discipline, planning, and integrity. He went on to develop multiple residential layouts, establishing a strong presence in the region's growing real estate landscape. Today, under his continued guidance alongside his sons Mr. Joy Sebastian and Dr. Philip Roy, the group is stronger than ever.

Mr. Mariadoss has always believed in creating opportunities — with a strong aspiration to generate employment, improve livelihoods, and support those in need. This philosophy continues to guide every venture's growth and its approach to business.
